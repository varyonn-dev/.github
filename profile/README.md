## 📱 Our Products

- **Pokett:** Our flagship B2C product. Pokett is an AI-powered financial management bot seamlessly integrated into WhatsApp, designed to help users track expenses, optimize budgets, and take control of their personal finances effortlessly.

## 🏗️ Engineering Philosophy & Architecture

We believe in **Quality by Design**. Because our products—like Pokett—handle sensitive financial data and rely on conversational AI, our engineering culture is built around:
- **Security & Privacy First:** Strict data governance and encryption standards to protect user financial data.
- **Cloud-Native Scalability:** Designing stateless, easily deployable microservices that can scale with WhatsApp message volume.
- **AI-Driven Automation:** Leveraging large language models and machine learning not just as buzzwords, but as core utilities to simplify complex user workflows.
- **Agile Delivery:** Iterative development, continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD), and a heavy emphasis on UX and design thinking.
